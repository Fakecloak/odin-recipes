This is Dosa.

INGREDIENTS

  • Step 1: Soak rice and urad dal (black gram) separately for 4-6 hours, then grind to a smooth batter.
  • Step 2: Ferment the batter overnight in a warm place.
  • Step 3: Heat a non-stick pan and pour a ladle of batter, spreading it into a thin circle.
  • Step 4: Drizzle oil around the edges and cook until golden brown, then flip and cook briefly.
  • Step 5: Serve hot with chutney and sambar.
